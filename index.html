Bachelor in Computer Technology

As technology becomes more advanced, computers and the ways we use them advance as well. With a bachelor's degree in computer science, computer information technology or computer engineering technology, you can get started learning about computers, their maintenance and how emerging technologies will change them. Online learning options are available for all of these degree programs.

Program Objective:

The Program Prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology. web mobile development, administration, and supervisoral skill to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specific object:

The learner should be able to:
  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experimental and field-based opportunities.

Job Opportunities

  • Visual graphics designer
  • Photographer
  • Video Editor
  • Front-end web developer
  • Back-end web developer
  • Full-stack web developer
  • System maintenance personnel
  • Teacher/Trainer
